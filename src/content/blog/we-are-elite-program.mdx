  <h1>Our unique approach to education</h1>

  <p>
    The We Are Elite Program is a customized curation program that helps students from the <strong>Cambridge education system</strong> successfully enter <strong>top US universities</strong>.
    We provide strategic support reflecting each student's goals and academic tendencies, and systematically manage the entire process from <strong>academics to extracurricular activities and application preparation</strong>.
  </p>

  <hr />

  <h2>Program Purpose and Philosophy</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Customized Learning Plan</strong>: Provide learning plans reflecting individual student goals and academic tendencies.</li>
    <li><strong>Bridge the Gap</strong>: Strategic support to bridge the differences between the Cambridge education system and the US college admission system.</li>
    <li><strong>Systematic Management</strong>: Systematically manage the entire process from academics to extracurricular activities and application preparation.</li>
    <li><strong>Emphasize Strengths</strong>: Create competitive college applications by emphasizing student strengths and uniqueness.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Initial Assessment and Goal Setting</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>
      <strong>Goal Analysis</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Set target universities and majors (e.g., STEM, Business, Humanities, etc.)</li>
        <li>Analyze admission requirements by university (SAT/ACT, TOEFL, GPA, extracurricular activities, etc.)</li>
        <li>Create Dream, Target, and Safety school lists</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Academic and Learning Style Assessment</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Evaluate current Cambridge grades (IGCSE, AS Level) and academic capabilities</li>
        <li>Identify learning style (creative, analytical, etc.) and interests</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ol>

  <h2>Maximizing Academic Achievement</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>
      <strong>Cambridge Subject Grade Management</strong><br />
      Target A/A* in AS/A-Level subjects. Optimize grades for conversion to the US university GPA system.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>US College Standardized Test Preparation</strong>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>SAT/ACT</strong>: Initial diagnostic test to assess ability and provide a customized learning plan.</li>
        <li><strong>AP Subject Learning</strong>: Select AP subjects that overlap with Cambridge subjects (e.g., Physics, Chemistry, Math, etc.).</li>
        <li><strong>TOEFL/IELTS</strong>: English proficiency test preparation and target score setting.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>College-Level Course Challenge</strong><br />
      Prepare for US universities through advanced Cambridge A-Level studies.
    </li>
  </ol>

  <h2>Extracurricular Activities and Leadership Enhancement</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>Customized Extracurricular Planning</strong>:<br />
      - Science Major: Research projects, Olympiad competition participation<br />
      - Social Sciences/Political Science Major: Model UN<br />
      - Creative individual projects and participation in international competitions (e.g., International Youth Mathematics Competition)
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Leadership Experience Enhancement</strong>:<br />
      - Lead school club activities<br />
      - Gain leadership experience in volunteer projects
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Portfolio Organization</strong>:<br />
      - Organize activity records, awards, photos, and videos for college applications.
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Admission Application Strategy</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>
      <strong>College Application Process Design</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Decide on Early Decision/Early Action</li>
        <li>Prepare Common App and UC Application</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Essay Writing and Editing</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Write a Common App essay emphasizing personal story</li>
        <li>Emphasize the connection between major and activities in Supplemental Essays</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Letter of Recommendation Management</strong><br />
      Request and support recommendation letters from Cambridge teachers and extracurricular mentors
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Interview Preparation</strong><br />
      Practice anticipated questions and responses, emphasizing Cambridge experience and adaptability to US universities
    </li>
  </ol>

  <h2>Timeline and Success Points</h2>
  <h3>High School Year 10</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Term 1-2</strong>: Standardized test preparation, building extracurricular and leadership experience</li>
    <li><strong>Term 3-4</strong>: Intensive SAT/ACT preparation, start AP subject learning, draft college list</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>High School Year 11</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Term 1-2</strong>: First SAT/ACT attempt, A-Level academic grade management</li>
    <li><strong>Term 3-4</strong>: AP exam taking, college visits, essay draft writing</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>High School Year 12</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Term 1-2</strong>: Preparation and submission of application documents</li>
    <li><strong>Term 3-4</strong>: College interviews, Waitlist response, and completion of scholarships and financial aid processes</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Program Success Factors</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Secure a strategic connection between the Cambridge education system and US university requirements</li>
    <li>Develop a portfolio that discovers and emphasizes each student's unique strengths</li>
    <li>Systematically prepare for standardized tests and university-specific essays</li>
    <li>Provide stable admission preparation support through regular feedback and mentoring</li>
  </ol>

  <h1>교육에 대한 우리만의 독특한 접근 방식</h1>

  <p>
    We Are Elite 프로그램은 <strong>캠브리지 교육 시스템</strong>을 받은 학생들이 <strong>미국 상위권 대학</strong>에 성공적으로 진학할 수 있도록 돕는 맞춤형 큐레이팅 프로그램입니다.
    학생 개개인의 목표와 학업 성향을 반영하여 전략적인 지원을 제공하며, <strong>학업부터 과외활동, 입시 서류 준비</strong>까지 전 과정을 체계적으로 관리합니다.
  </p>

  <hr />

  <h2>프로그램 목적 및 철학</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>맞춤형 학습 계획</strong>: 학생 개별 목표와 학업 성향을 반영한 학습 계획 제공.</li>
    <li><strong>격차 해소</strong>: 캠브리지 교육 시스템과 미국 대학 입시 체계 간의 차이를 메우는 전략적 지원.</li>
    <li><strong>체계적 관리</strong>: 학업, 과외활동, 입시 서류 준비까지 전 과정을 체계적으로 관리.</li>
    <li><strong>강점 강조</strong>: 학생의 강점과 독창성을 강조하여 경쟁력 있는 입학 지원서 작성.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>초기 진단 및 목표 설정</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>
      <strong>목표 분석</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>지원 희망 대학 및 전공 설정 (예: STEM, Business, Humanities 등)</li>
        <li>대학별 입학 조건 분석 (SAT/ACT, TOEFL, GPA, 과외활동 등)</li>
        <li>Dream, Target, Safety 대학 리스트 작성</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>학업 및 학습 성향 진단</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>현재 캠브리지 성적 (IGCSE, AS Level) 및 학업 역량 평가</li>
        <li>학습 스타일(창의형, 분석형 등) 및 관심사 확인</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ol>

  <h2>학업 성과 극대화</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>
      <strong>캠브리지 과목 성적 관리</strong><br />
      AS/A-Level 과목에서 A/A* 목표. 캠브리지 과목이 미국 대학 GPA 체계로 환산될 수 있도록 성적 최적화.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>미국 대학 표준 시험 대비</strong>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>SAT/ACT</strong>: 초반 진단 시험을 통해 실력 확인 및 맞춤형 학습 플랜 제공.</li>
        <li><strong>AP 과목 학습</strong>: 캠브리지 과목과 겹치는 AP 과목 선택 (예: Physics, Chemistry, Math 등).</li>
        <li><strong>TOEFL/IELTS</strong>: 영어 능력 시험 대비 및 목표 점수 설정.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>대학 수준 과목 도전</strong><br />
      캠브리지 A-Level 심화 학습을 통해 미국 대학 준비.
    </li>
  </ol>

  <h2>과외활동 및 리더십 강화</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>맞춤형 과외활동 기획</strong>:<br />
      - 과학 전공: 연구 프로젝트, 올림피아드 대회 참가<br />
      - 사회학/정치학 전공: Model UN<br />
      - 창의적인 개인 프로젝트와 국제 대회 참가 (예: 국제 청소년 수학 대회)
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>리더십 경험 강화</strong>:<br />
      - 학교 내 동아리 활동 주도<br />
      - 봉사활동 프로젝트 리더십 경험
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>포트폴리오 정리</strong>:<br />
      - 활동 기록, 수상 내역, 사진, 동영상을 정리하여 대학 지원에 활용.
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>입학 지원 전략</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>
      <strong>대학 지원 프로세스 설계</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Early Decision/Early Action 여부 결정</li>
        <li>Common App 및 UC Application 작성 준비</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>에세이 작성 및 편집</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>개인적인 스토리를 강조한 Common App 에세이 작성</li>
        <li>Supplemental Essay에서 전공과 활동의 연결성을 강조</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>추천서 관리</strong><br />

    <li>
      <strong>인터뷰 준비</strong><br />

    </li>
  </ol>

    <li><strong>Term 3-4</strong>: SAT/ACT 준비 심화, AP 과목 학습 시작, 대학 리스트 초안 작성</li>

  </ul>

  </ul>
